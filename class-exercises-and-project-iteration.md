I've decided to make an interactive self portrait of sorts, where the reader/player will journey through the 3 stages of life (birth, adolescence, and adulthood) through interactive poetry and choices. 
There are different ‘rooms’ where the reader can see a poem, with clickable choices at the end that parallel emotional logic during the respective stages. Some include the ‘firsts’ after being born, regretful choices and loops during the teen years, and hard decisions faced during adulthood. 
I have made a basic map (see below), with themes for each room, detailing branches, loops and convergent points. It is a pretty coherent story, using Javascript and html coding to create choices that reflect emotional consequences as opposed to mechanical responses. Chance is observed through the individual associations with each poem and stage, whereas ergodic effort lies within the regret loops, where re-reading a poem or passage may alter its meaning. Finally, visually I wish to keep it in black and white, using single-line drawings and a slowly growing garden which will turn green on the final page as a symbolism of growth and acceptance.
