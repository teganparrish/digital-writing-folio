#Bio

Hi, I’m Tegan. Primarily I write poetry, with a hint of short fiction when I feel like it. I use my trusty pink laptop to write down ideas and flesh them into more consumable literature. I am inspired by life - my own most of the time - and people around me, using this to create fictionalised versions of what has happened, or what could be. I feel a balance between the pen and paper versus my digital tools as even though it is a slower process, I feel more at ease and with myself when putting that ink on the paper. I am constrained by the light of the screen - I get tired, bored, distracted (the usual), but I push on when needed. Mostly from deadlines 😁Yet, this introduction to the digital world of writing makes me curious - can I learn a new format that will diminish that boredom and rather evoke digital passion that creates work? And where will these new skills lead me, I wonder?
